                  CONTROLS
arrows / wasd / hjkl : movement
                   o : open door
                   c : close door
                   g : get item from the ground
       t / backspace : throw item away
                   e : equip item
                   u : unequip item
                   f : attack closest monster
                   . : wait

                   TARGET
            Kill all the monsters.

               DAMAGE NUMBERS
2d6^+3 means roll 2 6-sided die with 3 rerolls.

               SPECIFICITIES
You can carry only one item. When dual wielding,
          you cannot carry anything.


           Press any key to continue
